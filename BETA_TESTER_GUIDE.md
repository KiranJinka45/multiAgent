# Welcome to the MultiAgent Beta! 🚀

Thank you for being one of our first 20 testers. You have been provisioned with a **Pro Tier** environment, giving you 50 missions per day and high-throughput API access.

## 🏁 Your First Mission
1. **Login**: Use the credentials provided in your invitation.
2. **Explore**: You'll see a "Welcome Guide" on your dashboard. This explains the core loop: **Goal -> Execution -> ROI**.
3. **Create Mission**: 
   - Click **"+ New Mission"**.
   - For your first mission, try something simple like: `Analyze the current project structure and suggest one optimization.`
4. **Watch it Work**: Navigate to the **Timeline** to see the agents collaborating in real-time.
5. **View Results**: Once completed, review the logs and the proposed changes.

## 📊 Understanding Your Dashboard
- **Missions**: Your operational command center.
- **Commercial Cockpit**: Tracks the **Dev Hours Saved** by using MultiAgent. Watch this grow as you complete more missions!
- **System Health**: Real-time status of the neural mesh.
